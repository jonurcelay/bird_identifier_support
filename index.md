### ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why wasn't my bird identified correctly?**  
A1: Try taking the photo with better lighting and a clearer view of the bird. Low confidence usually means the image is blurry or dark.
A2: If it's an audio, try recording again with less background noise and more clear sound from the bird. Low confidence usually means that the audio is not very clear or long enough.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**  
A: Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device.

**Q: Is the app accurate?**  
A: Our AI is highly accurate for common bird species, but results may vary with image quality.
